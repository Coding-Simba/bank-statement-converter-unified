Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges or credits.

Is there a free account for Pro and Business plans?

Yes! You can convert up to 2 bank statements per day without an account, or 5 per day with a free account. For higher volume needs, check out our paid plans starting at $30/month.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes! Save 20% when you pay annually. That's 2 months free compared to monthly billing.

What happens when I reach my conversion limit?

For Free plan users, you'll need to wait until the next month or upgrade to continue converting. Pro and Business plans have unlimited conversions.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time with no hidden fees or cancellation charges. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use bank-level encryption and never store your bank statements. All processing happens in memory and files are immediately deleted after conversion.
