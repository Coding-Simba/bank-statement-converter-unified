New Feature: Batch Processing for Multiple Statements

EP
Emily Park
Product Manager
December 28, 2024 3 min read

We're thrilled to announce one of our most requested features: batch processing for multiple bank statements! This powerful new capability allows you to convert an entire year's worth of statements—or even multiple years—in a single operation, saving you valuable time and effort.

What is Batch Processing?

Batch processing enables you to upload and convert multiple bank statement files simultaneously, rather than processing them one at a time. Whether you're preparing for tax season, conducting a financial audit, or simply organizing your records, this feature dramatically streamlines your workflow.

Key Benefits

  • Time Savings: Convert dozens or even hundreds of statements in minutes, not hours
  • Consistency: Apply the same conversion settings across all files automatically
  • Organization: Download all converted files in a single, organized ZIP file
  • Error Reduction: Minimize manual handling and potential mistakes

How It Works

Using our new batch processing feature is incredibly simple:

  1. Select Multiple Files: Click the new "Batch Upload" button on our homepage and select all the statement files you want to convert. You can select files from different months or even different banks.
  2. Configure Settings: Choose your preferred output format (CSV, Excel, QBO, etc.) and any additional conversion options. These settings will apply to all files in the batch.
  3. Process and Download: Click "Convert All" and our system will process your files simultaneously. Once complete, download all converted files in a convenient ZIP archive.

Supported Features

Multi-Bank Support

Process statements from different banks in the same batch—our intelligent recognition system handles each one appropriately.

Smart File Organization

Converted files are automatically organized by bank and date, making it easy to find what you need.

Secure Processing

All files are processed with the same bank-level security we use for individual conversions, with automatic deletion after download.

Progress Tracking

Monitor the conversion progress in real-time with our new batch processing dashboard.

Perfect for Tax Season

With tax season approaching, batch processing is the perfect tool for accountants, bookkeepers, and individuals who need to quickly organize their financial records. Convert an entire year of statements in minutes and have them ready for import into your accounting software.

"This feature has been our #1 user request, and we're excited to deliver a solution that saves our users hours of manual work," says Emily Park, Product Manager at BankCSVConverter. "We've designed it to be both powerful and easy to use, maintaining the simplicity our users love."

Availability and Pricing

Batch processing is now available to all users:

  • Free Users: Process up to 5 files per batch
  • Pro Users: Process up to 50 files per batch
  • Business Users: Unlimited batch processing with priority processing

The feature is rolling out today and will be available to all users by the end of the week. No action is required on your part—simply log in to your account to access the new batch upload option.

What's Next?

This is just the beginning. We're already working on enhancements to batch processing, including:

  • Scheduled batch processing for recurring conversions
  • Advanced filtering and sorting options
  • Integration with cloud storage services
  • Batch analysis and reporting features

We're committed to making BankCSVConverter the most efficient tool for managing your financial data. Stay tuned for more exciting updates!
