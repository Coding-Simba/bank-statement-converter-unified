Step 1: Prepare Your PDF Bank Statement

Before converting, ensure your PDF meets these requirements:

  • PDF is not password-protected (unlock it first if needed)
  • Text is selectable (not a scanned image)
  • File size is under 50MB
  • Statement is from a supported bank (we support 1000+ banks)

Bank-Specific Conversion Tips

Chase statements are well-structured and convert easily. Tips:

  • Download statements directly from Chase Online for best results
  • Credit card statements include merchant categories automatically
  • Business accounts may have additional transaction details

Bank of America PDFs work seamlessly. Note that:

  • Check images are excluded from the CSV export
  • Pending transactions are marked with an asterisk
  • International transactions include exchange rates

Wells Fargo statements convert with these features:

  • Reference numbers are preserved for each transaction
  • Daily balance summaries are included
  • ATM locations are extracted when available

Troubleshooting Common Issues

"Cannot read PDF" Error

Cause: PDF is image-based or corrupted

Solution: Use our OCR feature or re-download the statement from your bank

Password-Protected PDF

Cause: Bank security settings

Solution: Remove password using Adobe Reader or online tools first

Missing Transactions

Cause: Multi-page formatting issues

Solution: Ensure all pages are included in the PDF

Wrong Date Format

Cause: Regional settings

Solution: Use our date format selector in advanced options

Best Practices for PDF to CSV Conversion

1. Download Fresh Statements

Always download the latest version from your bank's website rather than using email attachments, which may be compressed or altered.

2. Check Date Ranges

Ensure your statement covers the full period you need. Some banks split long statements into multiple files.

3. Verify Totals

After conversion, verify that the opening and closing balances match your PDF statement.

4. Regular Backups

Keep both PDF and CSV versions for your records. CSVs are great for analysis, PDFs for official documentation.
