Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we protect your financial data with industry-leading security measures.

Last Updated: January 26, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At BankCSVConverter, we understand that your financial data is highly sensitive. We've built our service with privacy and security at its core. This policy explains how we handle your information with the utmost care and transparency.

Key Privacy Features

  • All files are automatically deleted after processing
  • We never store your bank statements or financial data
  • No account required for basic conversions
  • Bank-level encryption for all data transfers
  • GDPR and CCPA compliant

Information We Collect

1. Files You Upload

When you use our conversion service:

  • Bank statement PDFs or images are processed in memory
  • Files are automatically deleted immediately after conversion
  • We do not retain copies of your financial documents
  • No transaction data is stored in our databases

2. Account Information (Optional)

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address for login and communication
  • Password (encrypted using bcrypt)
  • Usage statistics (number of conversions, not content)
  • Billing information for paid plans (processed by Stripe)

3. Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data to improve our service:

  • Conversion success rates
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Error logs (without file content)
  • Page views and navigation patterns

How We Protect Your Data

End-to-End Encryption

All data transfers use TLS 1.3 encryption, ensuring your files are protected in transit.

Secure Processing

Files are processed in isolated containers and never written to permanent storage.

Auto-Deletion

All uploaded files are automatically deleted within seconds of processing.

No Data Mining

We never analyze, sell, or share your financial data with third parties.

Your Rights and Controls

Data Access and Portability

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Export your account data in a portable format
  • Know what information we collect and why

Data Deletion

You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Since we don't store your financial documents, there's nothing to delete after conversion.

Marketing Communications

We only send emails related to your account and service updates. You can opt out of all non-essential communications in your account settings.

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies necessary for the service to function:

  • Session cookies: To maintain your login state
  • Preference cookies: To remember your settings
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous usage data (can be disabled)

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels from third-party advertisers.

Third-Party Services

We use carefully selected third-party services:

  • Stripe: For secure payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • AWS: For secure cloud infrastructure
  • Cloudflare: For DDoS protection and SSL certificates

These providers are bound by strict data processing agreements and cannot access your financial documents.

Compliance and Certifications

BankCSVConverter complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI-DSS)
  • SOC 2 Type II certification (in progress)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through a prominent notice on our website.
