GDPR Compliance

Your data rights are protected. Complete transparency, full control, and unwavering commitment to European data protection standards.

Last Updated: January 26, 2025 | GDPR Compliant Since: May 25, 2018

Our GDPR Commitment

BankCSVConverter is fully committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We've built our service with privacy by design, ensuring your financial data is protected at every step.

Key GDPR Principles We Follow

  • Lawfulness, fairness, and transparency in all data processing
  • Purpose limitation - we only use data for stated purposes
  • Data minimization - we collect only what's necessary
  • Accuracy - we ensure data is correct and up to date
  • Storage limitation - automatic deletion after processing
  • Integrity and confidentiality - bank-level security
  • Accountability - we document and prove compliance

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject under GDPR, you have comprehensive rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you, how we use it, and who we share it with.

Right to Rectification

Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data when it's no longer necessary for the purpose collected.

Right to Restrict

Request limitation of processing your data while we verify accuracy or investigate your concerns.

Right to Portability

Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format to transfer to another service.

Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests, direct marketing, or research purposes.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data only when we have a valid legal basis:

Contract Performance

Processing necessary to provide our bank statement conversion service as requested by you.

Consent

For optional features like marketing communications, we obtain your explicit consent.

Legal Obligations

When required by law, such as tax records or anti-money laundering regulations.

Legitimate Interests

For improving our service and security, always balanced against your rights and freedoms.

Data We Collect & Process

Personal Data Categories

  • Identity Data: Name, username (if account created)
  • Contact Data: Email address, communication preferences
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage Data: How you use our service, feature preferences
  • Financial Documents: Bank statements (processed only, never stored)

Special Category Data

We do NOT collect or process any special category data (racial/ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, genetic data, biometric data, health data, or data about sex life or sexual orientation).

Data Protection Measures

We implement state-of-the-art technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

Encryption

TLS 1.3 for transfers, AES-256 for any temporary storage

Anonymization

Usage analytics are fully anonymized and aggregated

Auto-Deletion

Financial documents deleted within seconds of processing

Access Control

Strict access controls and regular security audits

Data Retention Periods

  • Bank Statements: Deleted immediately after processing (typically within 30 seconds)
  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Communication Records: 2 years from last contact
  • Technical Logs: 90 days for security and debugging
  • Legal Records: As required by applicable law (typically 6 years)

International Transfers

Your data is primarily processed within the European Economic Area (EEA). If we need to transfer data outside the EEA, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • EU-approved Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission
  • Your explicit consent for specific transfers
  • Binding Corporate Rules for intra-group transfers

Data Breach Procedures

In the unlikely event of a data breach:

  • We will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours
  • If the breach poses high risk to your rights, we'll notify you directly
  • We maintain detailed breach logs and response procedures
  • Regular security drills ensure rapid, effective response

Your Right to Complain

If you're not satisfied with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with:

  • Our Data Protection Officer: dpo@bankcsvconverter.com
  • Your local supervisory authority: Find your authority at edpb.europa.eu
  • The Irish Data Protection Commission: Our lead supervisory authority

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 16, we will promptly delete it.

Updates to GDPR Compliance

We regularly review and update our GDPR compliance measures. Any material changes will be communicated to you via email or prominent notice on our website at least 30 days before taking effect.
