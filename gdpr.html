General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

Bank Statement Converter is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data Controller

Bank Statement Converter

Your Rights Under GDPR

  • Right to Access: You can request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request limitation of processing
  • Right to Data Portability: You can receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain types of processing

Data We Collect

  • Email address (if you create an account)
  • Usage data and analytics (anonymized)
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type)
  • Bank statements (temporarily processed, never stored)

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide our conversion service
  • To improve our service quality
  • To communicate with you about your account
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transfers
  • Secure data centers with ISO 27001 certification
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Immediate deletion of uploaded files after processing
  • No permanent storage of bank statements

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary:

  • Account data: Until account deletion
  • Bank statements: Deleted immediately after conversion
  • Usage logs: 90 days
  • Legal records: As required by law

International Transfers

Your data is processed within the European Economic Area (EEA). Any transfers outside the EEA are protected by appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses.
