About Bank Statement Converter

Transforming financial data management since 2018, we're on a mission to make bank statement processing simple, secure, and efficient for businesses worldwide.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a team of financial technology experts, Bank Statement Converter was born from a simple observation: businesses were spending countless hours manually processing bank statements. We knew there had to be a better way.


What started as a small project to help local businesses has grown into a comprehensive platform serving thousands of companies globally. Our AI-powered technology now processes millions of transactions monthly, saving businesses time and reducing errors in their financial workflows.
